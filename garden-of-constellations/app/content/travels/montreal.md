# Montreal

Montreal feels like a city of contrasts.

Cold winters shape the rhythm of life here,
while summers bring loud streets, festivals,
and long evenings by the water.

## First impressions

The first thing you notice is the mix of languages.
French and English blend into everyday life.

## Atmosphere

Old stone buildings sit beside modern glass towers.
It feels layered—like different eras stacked together.

## Moments

- Quiet metro rides in winter
- Warm cafés during snowstorms
- Street festivals in summer
- Walking without a fixed destination

---

Montreal doesn’t feel like a single mood.
It changes depending on how you move through it.
